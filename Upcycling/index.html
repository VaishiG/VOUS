Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What is upcycling?
A: Upcycling is the process of transforming old or unwanted materials into something new and useful.
Q: How long does the upcycling process take?
A: The duration depends on various factors such as the complexity of the design and current workload. On average, it may take 2-4 weeks.
Q: Do you accept clothes with stains or minor damages?
A: We recommend submitting clothes in good condition as it ensures better results. However, minor stains or damages can sometimes be addressed during the upcycling process.
